Land Banking

This is the oldest practice of real eatate and one of the most profitable form of real estate engagement. It is the acquisition of small or large expanse of land to hold for some time and then selling it at profit. It is a long term investment of up to 5 years and above. Investors have the motive to resale the underdeveloped land intending tobprofit from it. Simply mean investor gets the land, bank it till substantial infrastructure development or population growth in the areas causes the demand which affect the price of the land It is good if you’re running on a low budget which aim at fulfilling the concept of demand and supply.
